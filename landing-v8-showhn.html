How It Works

Four steps. No intermediaries.

1

Create a contract

Pick a template. Set scope, milestones, deadlines, and payment terms. Both parties sign.

2

Client funds escrow

Funds lock on XRPL with crypto-conditions. Not in our bank account -- on the ledger, verifiable by both parties.

3

Deliver and approve

Submit work per milestone. Client reviews and approves. SHA-256 hash of each deliverable is recorded on-chain.

4

Funds release + MCC mints

Escrow releases instantly on approval. A Minted Craft Credential is issued -- verifiable proof of completed work.

Under the Hood

Built for people who read the source.

XRPL Token Escrow (XLS-85)

Native escrow objects on the XRP Ledger. Not a smart contract wrapper -- a first-class ledger primitive. Supports time-based cancellation and crypto-conditions.

Crypto-Conditions (SHA-256 preimage)

Escrow release requires a SHA-256 preimage fulfillment via five-bells-condition (RFC draft-thomas-crypto-conditions). Platform holds the preimage until approval.

Multi-Currency Trust Lines

RLUSD (Ripple-issued, NYDFS regulated), USDC, USDT, USD, EUR via GateHub issued currencies. RLUSD-only for escrow contracts to eliminate volatility.

XLS-20 NFTokens for Credentials

MCCs are XLS-20 tokens with 3 taxons: Work Credential (1), License (2), Access Pass (3). Metadata references contract hash, deliverable IPFS URI, and license terms.

AES-256-GCM Encryption

Platform-managed wallet seeds and escrow preimages encrypted at rest. Per-key IV, authenticated encryption. Key rotation planned for production.

AUSTRAC VASP Compliant

Registered under the AML/CTF Amendment Act 2024 (Tranche 2). Full AML/CTF program drafted. Travel Rule compliance for all virtual asset transfers. No minimum threshold.

Open Source

MIT license. Next.js 14 + TypeScript + xrpl.js v4. Supabase for auth and data. The code is the documentation. github.com/studioledger

Chain-Agnostic Wallet Connect

Xaman (XRPL), MetaMask (EVM), Phantom (Solana), GemWallet, Crossmark. Bring any wallet. Settlement always runs on XRPL.
