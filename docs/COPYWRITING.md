## Key Messaging Pillars

### Pillar 1: Payment Guarantee
**Message:** "Get paid first, deliver second."
**Evidence:** Escrow-backed contracts on XRPL
**Where to use:** All ads, hero section, email

### Pillar 2: Verifiable Proof
**Message:** "Your work deserves on-chain proof."
**Evidence:** MCC credentials, portable reputation
**Where to use:** Social, email, profile pages

### Pillar 3: Zero Intermediary
**Message:** "The blockchain IS the settlement layer."
**Evidence:** Direct XRPL payment, no platform holding funds
**Where to use:** Long-form, pitch decks, regulatory comms

### Pillar 4: Lowest Fee
**Message:** "0.98% fee. Everyone else is 10%+."
**Evidence:** Upwork (10%), Fiverr (20%), Braintrust (15%)
**Where to use:** Comparison pages, competitive ads, trust signals

### Pillar 5: Momentum Through Reputation
**Message:** "Build reputation that compounds across jobs."
**Evidence:** MCC credentials accumulate, visible to all future clients
**Where to use:** Retention, repeat booking messaging

## Tone Guardrails

### ✅ DO
- **Bold, direct headlines** ("Stop chasing payment")
- **Specific numbers** (0.98%, AUD $5K, 2 weeks)
- **Action verbs** (get, deliver, build, close, earn)
- **Problem-first** (what we solve, then how)
- **Transparent** (fees, blockchain, how it works)

### ❌ DON'T
- **Crypto jargon** (no "blockchain for creatives" — say "escrow-backed")
- **Corporate speak** ("synergize", "leverage", "ecosystem")
- **Vagueness** (avoid "industry-leading", "innovative")
- **Overpromising** (we solve payment + negotiation, not discovery)
- **Mentioning XRPL to users** (they don't care about tech, only results)

## Notes for Future Iterations

- **A/B test payment vs reputation messaging** — payment likely higher converting, but reputation builds long-term retention
- **Test "no credit card required" prominently** — TIES uses it, low friction signal
- **Test "free profile" vs "free trial"** — semantic difference but might matter
- **Consider pricing transparency** — land on fees early (0.98%) builds trust
- **MCC credentials might confuse early users** — maybe keep simple until post-signup education
